What is PhoenixCraft?

Phoenixcraft, is a Minecraft Towny server.

PhoenixCraft is community driven, individual focused, and thrives on player interaction. Experiences are just waiting to be had! Come on in!

Features

Features

Towny

Towny allows for the creation of towns and nations. Fully customizable with names, townboards and town ranks!

DynMap

Dynmap allows for the remote view of the server map via web browser. Useful if you don't want to poke with map mods like JourneyMap or Xaero's Minimap.

Dynmap is accessable via MineMap.gsheps.net

Server Economy

PhoenixCraft features a player-interactive economy based on mob-drops and food goods. Players can sell these goods for in-game currency to purchase items, or to grow their towns!

Independent Play

If founding a town, or joining a town isn't your thing, there's always independent play! PhoenixCraft also offers the ability to use Grief-Prevention to protect your land if you choose not to do either of those things.
